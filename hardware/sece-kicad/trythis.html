The SECE Framework: Technical Abstract

The Synthetic Emotional Cognition Engine (SECE) operates on the principle of Weighted Resonance. Traditional AI often fails in high‑stakes senior care because it treats emotional input as simple data points. SECE introduces a dynamic scoring system that evaluates signals through three critical filters:

Primary Equation: Resonance = I × C × R

  • (I) Intensity: The amplitude of the stimulus relative to the baseline.
  • (C) Clarity: The signal‑to‑noise ratio in a complex home environment.
  • (R) Relevance: The alignment with the core directive of “Care Stability.”
