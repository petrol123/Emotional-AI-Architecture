# Invention Overview  
### SECE Core + Portable Knowledge Base  
### Inventor: Paul Totoritis

This document establishes the conceptual origin and authorship of a two‑tier emotional and knowledge architecture for AI systems.

## Purpose
To provide AI assistants with:
- a compact analog emotional engine (SECE)
- a portable, swappable, offline knowledge module (PKB)

## Problem
AI systems lack emotional intelligence and rely heavily on cloud access. This limits their usefulness in clinical and home‑care environments.

## Solution
A hybrid system combining:
- SECE for emotional reasoning  
- MedGemma for clinical reasoning  
- PKB for offline medical knowledge  

## Applications
Healthcare, elder care, home visits, rural medicine, and any offline environment.
